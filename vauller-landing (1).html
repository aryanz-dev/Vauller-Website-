The problem

Every missed call is a missed pour.

Dispatch lines ring off the hook, orders get taken on scraps of paper, and details still have to be re-keyed into Command Alkon by hand.

01

Calls go unanswered

When the line's busy, the order — and the customer — goes to a competitor.

02

Manual order entry

Details are captured by hand, then typed again into your system. Two chances to get it wrong.

03

No room to scale

More volume means more staff on the phones — and slower nights and weekends.

Answered, captured, synced — automatically.
